# The Next Dev Portal

A rapid developer portal building tool based on [React](https://reactjs.org/), 
[Nextjs](https://nextjs.org/), [MDX](https://mdxjs.com/), and [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com).

## The Next Dev Portal is

- **Easy to install, customize and author** - Developers can use their editor of choice to author markdown documents.
- **Fast and secure** - Statically generated, lightweight.
- **Fun to use** - Supports TypeScript and MDX.
