Our new website version 2.0 is up!

The original website was built on [eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/) which I got fond due to its flexibility. Then I found [MDX](https://mdxjs.com/).

The new website is built on NextJS, which allows MDX + SSG combination.

Our product [EcoLoop](https://www.ecoloop.io) is also built on Nextjs hence it was a natural choice to use the same tech stack.
