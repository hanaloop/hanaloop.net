This webinar will help managers and executives understand how climate change impacts business and operations, as well as identify key strategies to adapt and stay competitive in the new carbon-neutral era.

## Webinar Content

- Impact of Climate Change on Industry: How climate risks and market pressure are reshaping business competitiveness
- Climate Regulations: Key international standards governing greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions management at both corporate (Scopes 1, 2, and 3) and product levels
- Preparing for the Future: Leveraging digital solutions to manage and reduce GHG emissions

By Young-Suk Ahn Park, MSeng
- Date: Thursday, November 13, 2025
- Time: 4:00 p.m. (EST, Panama)

<!--truncate-->

### Objective

The objective of this webinar is to provide executives and professionals with a clear understanding of how climate change and related regulations affect businesses,
and to present practical management and decarbonization steps that enable companies to thrive in a business environment where carbon has become a key factor of competitiveness.

### Audience

This webinar is designed especially for executives and sustainability leaders who wish to strengthen their capabilities in carbon emissions data management,
covering both corporate and value chain carbon footprints (Scopes 1, 2, and 3), as well as product carbon footprints.
It is also highly relevant for consulting, technology, and other professionals interested in corporate decarbonization.

### About the Speaker

Young-Suk Ahn Park is the CTO of HanaLoop, leading the development of sustainability and data management platforms.

With over 20 years of experience in management consulting and digital innovation for global companies,
he has advised numerous organizations on implementing sustainability and digital transformation strategies.

He has been invited as a speaker at international workshops and conferences in countries such as Panama, Brazil, Nepal, the Philippines, and Korea.
