Cloud-based carbon management solution leader, HanaLoop Corp, signed a supply contract last December with Byucksan, a company subject to emissions trading, for its carbon accounting solution, HanaEco. After successfully piloting the SaaS-based carbon management solution "HanaEco" for the past three months, it has been officially introduced and stabilized across all business sites in March.
